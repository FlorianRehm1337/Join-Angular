What is Join?

Join is a kanban project managment tool that helps you organize and manage your tasks

How to use it?

1.

First, create a task in the add-task section. Choose a title, description and deadline. Assign your team members to the task and choose or create a category. Choose an urgency to quickly see the priority of any task. Optionally create subtasks to divide up your task in smaller steps and therefore see progress easier.

2.

After creating, your task is moved to the board. You can also filter your tasks in the board section. So you can see urgent tasks a lot faster. You can also add a new Task in the board section aswell.

3.

Move your tasks by dragging them into the different columns to change their status. Complete your subtasks to see your progress. See detailed information by clicking on a task. Edit your task when necesarry and delete it when it's done or not needed anymore.
